"My Best friend"

Today I want to talk about my friend. Her name is Minji, and she is 22 years old. Even though she is not a teenager, she is childish. I have known her since 2022. We have a really great relationship because we both enjoy lots of the same things. For example, we both listen to music, play games, and count on each other. Although she is my good friend, I don't even see her. She is important to me because she gives me many ideas, listens to my voice, and heals my feelings when I feel down. That's why she is my best and most important friend.
